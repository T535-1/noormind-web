Enlightenment Psychology in Light of AI and Metaverse Technologies

This paper aims to present a new vision of how Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Metaverse technologies can be employed to develop and enhance the methodology of Enlightenment Therapy, which includes enhancing mindfulness, invoking divine light, and deepening the sense of being in the presence of God...

The research concludes by providing future recommendations on how AI and Metaverse technologies can be developed to become more effective tools that support Enlightenment Therapy, while also considering ethical and religious aspects of these applications.

Keywords: Enlightenment Psychology, AI, Metaverse, Meditation, Spiritual Therapy, AR.
